Rules of Senior Assassin

(Disclaimer! : By paying to be in this game, you are agreeing to all of the following rules. Breaking any rule will result in disqualification)

How to Play:

For a legitimate “assassination,” video proof must be sent to both @siseniorassasin (Instagram) Team captains are chosen internally and are unknown to the rest of the teams participating; therefore, all members are considered potential targets. No repeated Captains.

Weapons:

  • Only WATER-based weapons (water guns, hoses, water balloons, water bottles…etc)
  • No Nerf Guns
  • No Splatterguns

Off-limit Zones

School

Anywhere on the SI campus is strictly prohibited. Sidewalk in front of school (37th ave from Rivera to Pacheco).

Homes

  • You may NOT break into anyone’s home, even if you know how.
  • You can only enter a target’s home if a parent, legal guardian or trusted friend gives permission. The home includes a person’s garage and/or backyard
  • If any adult tells you to leave any time, do so IMMEDIATELY. This means getting completely off of their property. No hiding behind a car until they go back into their house.

Work

Assassinations are not allowed in your target's workplace. Your target becomes safe as soon as he/she walks into his/her workplace, walking through the parking lot before or after is, however, fair game. (volunteer work is included) No breaking any laws! (do you want to get arrested?)
